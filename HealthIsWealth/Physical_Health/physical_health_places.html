Physical Healthcare Facilities
Polyclinics

Polyclinics are healthcare centres that provide primary care such as medical treatment, preventive healthcare, and health education, at a subsidised cost

Hospitals

Hospitals provides affordable specialist care for patients, where you can go to when you are facing any medical conditions that cannot be treated normally at general clinics.

General Practitioners

General practitioners are doctors who provide nonsurgical medical care. General practitioners evaluate patients through exams and tests and diagnose conditions such as diseases or injuries.

Dental

A Dentist is responsible for performing oral surgery and routine cleanings on patients. They repair and remove teeth, diagnose conditions and tell patients about how to best care for their teeth and gums.

Dermatologists

A dermatologist specializes in conditions that affect the skin, hair, and nails. Whether it's rashes or wrinkles, no one understands your skin, hair, and nails better than a board-certified dermatologist.

Pharmacies

Places like Watsons, Unity or Guardian. If you know what medicine to get for your symptoms such as paracetemol, visit these places to get it over the counter
